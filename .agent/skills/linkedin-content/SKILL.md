# LinkedIn Content Creation Skill

This skill outlines the specific requirements, tone, and formatting for creating LinkedIn posts for this project.

## 1. Core Brand Identity
*   **Product Name**: Always refer to the project as **"LinkedIn Agent"** (formerly "Nexton").
*   **Perspective**: Always use the **First Person ("I", "My", "Me")**. Do not use "We" or "Our".

## 2. Tone & Style
*   **Professional yet Personal**: Share the *engineering journey*, not just the result.
*   **Transparent**: Admit challenges (e.g., "The black box problem", "The wall of text").
*   **Technical but Accessible**: Use technical terms (FastAPI, SSE, React) but explain *why* they matter to the UX.
*   **Hashtags**: Use relevant tags like `#FastAPI #React #AI #BuildInPublic #Engineering`.
